Empanadas Caseras

Description

Empanadas Caseras are a national dish from Argentina.

Some World class Chefs say that this dish is on the top 10 most loved foods of the world! Flour-based dough, various forms of fillings, cubed or ground beef, ham, chicken, fish or cheese, hard-boiled eggs, olives, peppers, and onions folded into the mixture.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 hard-boiled large eggs, chopped
  • 1/3 cup chopped pitted green olives
  • tablespoon ground cumin
  • tablespoon chili powder
  • teaspoon smoked paprika
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 packages (14 oz. each) frozen empanada dough discs, thawed
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • Salsa verde, optional

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and onion
  2. Stir in the eggs, olives and spices
  3. Brush egg over the edges of the dough circles and add the filling
  4. Fold dough over the filling, crimp and seal
  5. Brush the tops with egg wash
  6. Bake the empanadas at CÂº 180
  7. Serve!
