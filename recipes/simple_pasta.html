Simple Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the garlic mixture. Toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
